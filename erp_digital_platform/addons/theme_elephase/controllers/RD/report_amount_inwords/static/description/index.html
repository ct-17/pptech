Display The Total Amount In Words on Your P.O, S.O, Quote and Invoice

Make your business documents compliant with the requirement to display the total amount in words

Amount in Words

This App will automatically convert the invoice amount, quote amount, sales order amount and purchase order amount in figures into words. The amount in words can be displayed in 13 different languages.The langauge setting of the customer/partner/supplier will determine which language to use. If language of partner/customer/supplier is not supported, it defaults to english.The default language can be changed if you request us to do it:

  • en (English, default)
  • fr (French)
  • de (German)
  • es (Spanish)
  • lt (Lithuanian)
  • lv (Latvian)
  • en_GB (British English)
  • en_IN (Indian English)
  • no (Norwegian)
  • pl (Polish)
  • ru (Russian)
  • dk (Danish)
  • pt_BR (Brazilian Portuguese)
Note that it is possible to disable this feature if you don't want it to appear in the PDF reports.

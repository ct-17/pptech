Odoo Multi Seller Marketplace

If you want to create your own marketplace like Amazon on your odoo website this is the module for you, Which enables you to convert your odoo ecommerce website into complete multi vendor/seller marketplace.

Click here for the video tutorial

key features:

  • Marketplace supports multiple Seller.
  • Multiple seller can sign up and can sell products on your website.
  • Marketplace helps to manage product's stock information.
  • Seller can view their own profile with products.
  • Admin can view all seller details with products approved/non-approved products and stock details.
  • Approved and published products are displayed on website.
